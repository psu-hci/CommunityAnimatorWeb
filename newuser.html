Terms of Use and Conditions

Researchers at PSU have collaborated with the New Leaf Initiative to learn more about how community members will use this app and how technology would influence social interaction and engagement. By using this application, you agree to share data from your experiences and uses of the app although you can opt out of this study at any time.

Your information will be kept confidential in accordance with to HIPAA standards. Any data that is used in publications be aggregated with other users information and will not include any information that will personally identify you as a user.

For further information on this study, please contact Dr. Jess Kropczynski (jessk@psu.edu) or Dr. Jack Carroll (jmcarroll@psu.edu).
