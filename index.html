What is Community Animator?

Traditionally, a ‘community animator’ is a person that connects people looking for partners on community conversations and projects. The app works much like a dating app. Instead of matching people who want a date, it connects people who want to come together to do something for their community.

Community Animator is currently being developed by The Center for Human-Computer Interaction at Penn State.
