What is Community Animator?

Community Animator aims to provide a way for people to meet up, and also to study how people interact. It is currently being developed by The Center for Human-Computer Interaction at Penn State along with a team from IST 413: Usability Engineering.

live_help

How do I use Community Animator?

By following these steps:

  • Step 1: Create an account
  • Step 2: Create a profile that lists your interest areas
  • Step 3: Look for others nearby with similar interests
  • Step 4: Connect with others near you with the same interests
